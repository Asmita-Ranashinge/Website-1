Shiv Malhar Educational Software Services Pvt. Ltd. MUMBAI

As per the name itself, is a software development company especially related with the development of software in educational sector. A team of highly educated staff in various sectors are striving hard to make the mission possible and share their dreams along with our software development team.
DIGITIZATION OF EDUCATION is the prime motto of our company and is bounded to enable the education sector to reach each an individual user in a friendly and transparent way. As the world next door is growing fast with the help of super technologies, we are also bound to keep our stake high in building a strong educated nation by DIGITIZING THE EXISTING EDUCATIONAL SYSTEM.
In this process, our company has come up with a revolutionary software called 'DEN' (DIGITIZED EDUCATIONAL NETWORK) keeping it in a very user friendly way followed up with intensive backup services and strong technical support, online and offline, within an economical reach of each and every user.
