Product Modules

School Management

School can add end number of institutes capturing all information and details, regarding institutes, boards and medium as well as helps in managing the institute, class, sections, exam.

User Management

This Section allows the administrator to add or delete the entire information regarding all the users existing in the system. This helps to maintain the database.

Admission Management

This module allows easy access for online and semi online admission. Real time admission status will be displayed on dashboard.

Communication Management

This is powerful module which connects parents, teachers and non-academic staff with school on a single click. This helps the users to exchange their data with each other in a simple and automatic way with personal security.

Fees

Fees status, due date, late fees, and Fees defaulters could be listed here. Fees structure can be defined by admin for each standard.

Timetable

This module helps in creation of the dynamic timetable, lecture management, recess management etc. in an automated way with defined structure.

Exam/Result Management

This module will help to implement the examination process in the existing system including paper setting, seating arrangement and results. Students can view their results on their personal dashboard instantly.

Attendance Management

This gives an accuracy regarding attendance of all teaching and non-teaching staff and the students in an automated way and can be easily viewed by the administration and parents as well.

E-mail Integration

This feature helps the user to convey their mails / messages instantly and connects through their personal mail account automatically.

Schedule Management

Schedule management facility is very useful for teaching staff so that they can easily create, save or submit their sessions to the admin for approval.

Leave Management

This Module allows you to keep a detailed track of leaves available, used and pending. It allows the Sub admin or faculty to file his application online for approval instantly.

Daily Diary Management

Parents will be kept updated about their wards’ performance and related information like remarks, attendance, complaints, homework and over view.

Library

This is the expansive and elaborated module managing Library independently. This library module is elaborated with E-Book facilities. It helps in keeping the exact records regarding availability and requirement of books in the library automatically.

Transportation Management

This module helps the parents as well as the administration to ensure the safety and security of children’s and gives the exact location of their school vehicle as the same itself is connected directly with the GPS system.

Inventory Management

This module helps to manage all the educational materials such as stationary, computers, books, uniform etc. and any other types mentioned by the school.

Holiday Muster Management

This module helps to design the academic sessions of holidays at your organization as per fixed holidays and yearly holidays.

E-Locker Management

This module works like a locker which secures the entire documentation regarding staff, students, administration, and can be easily procured whenever required instantly. These documents are saved for years. This helps in reducing the paperwork too.

Customizable Dashboard

This feature allows you to customize the existing pattern in the system such as, HOW and WHAT type of reports / data is to be required.

Free School Website

This feature allows you to establish your own website at the cost of our company which naturally gives your school a global identity.

Master Report

This feature generates the required reports of any size(Large or Small) on a single button click. This naturally reduces the sorting efforts and time.

School Application

This is a revolutionary feature which helps the user in keeping access to this software through their individual smart phones from any place at any time.

Alumni

Students, past and present, are what any education system should be all about. Alumni offers valuable insights and connections and also helps you to refine your school network.

E-Book

This module is specially design for student so that they can carry fewer books. Students, teachers have facility to download the e-book for corresponding subjects.

HR/Payroll

This module helps in keeping the actual status / data regarding all the teaching and non teaching staff, students etc. and keeps a good interaction between each other.
